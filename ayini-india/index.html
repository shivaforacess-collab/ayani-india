Why We Exist

Every Bite Should Be Safe. Every Business Should Be Confident.

In today's globalized, fast-moving food industry, rising consumer awareness, tightening regulation, and recurring contamination incidents have made science-driven food safety solutions essential. AYINI India was built to be a proactive force in meeting that need — offering trust to producers, retailers and the consumers who depend on them.

Public Health First

Foodborne illness affects millions globally each year. We help businesses prevent contamination, ensure hygiene, and comply with safety standards that protect communities.

Rising Regulatory Demands

Governments and international bodies keep tightening food safety regulation. We guide businesses through HACCP, ISO 22000, FSSAI and more, as a trusted certification partner.

Growing Consumer Awareness

Today's consumers demand transparency and safety. Our services help producers and retailers build trust and brand loyalty by proving their commitment to safety.

A Growing Market

From restaurants to manufacturing and packaging, every segment of the food industry needs safety expertise — creating a sustainable, scalable opportunity to serve.

Technology-Led Solutions

IoT, digital monitoring and AI are transforming food safety. We integrate modern, tech-enabled solutions into every safety protocol we design.

Social Responsibility

Beyond compliance, we aim to reduce foodborne risk, reduce food waste, and support sustainable food systems that protect vulnerable populations.

Our Mission

To protect public health and strengthen the global food chain by delivering expert consulting, innovative research, science-driven solutions and unwavering commitment to compliance, quality, trust, sustainability, and excellence — from farm to fork.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted global partner in food innovation and advancing food safety excellence — pioneering research, shaping global standards, and driving transformative solutions so every bite is safe, every business is compliant, and every community is protected.
