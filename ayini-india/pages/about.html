About AYINI India

An Indian-Born Partner in Food Safety, Compliance & R&D

Who We Are

We Don't Just Meet Standards — We Raise Them

AYINI India Private Limited is an Indian-born, niche food safety consulting organization and a trusted partner with 30+ years of cumulative experience with deep roots in industry best practices.

We are not just offering a service — we are offering trust. Trust to food producers that they are meeting international standards. Trust to retailers that their shelves are safe. And, most importantly, trust to the consumers who deserve to know that their meals are safe to eat. Through innovation, compliance and education, we are committed to being a catalyst for that transformation.

Our Mission

To protect public health and strengthen the global food chain by delivering expert consulting, innovative research, science-driven solutions and unwavering commitment to compliance, quality, trust, sustainability, and excellence — from farm to fork.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted global partner in food innovation and advancing food safety excellence — pioneering research, shaping global standards, and driving transformative solutions so every bite is safe, every business is compliant, and every community is protected.

Our Quality Policy

At AYINI India, we are committed to delivering uncompromising quality in food safety solutions that protect public health, ensure regulatory compliance, and foster trust across the food supply chain. We provide accurate, reliable, science-based services; meet and exceed client expectations through solutions aligned with national and international standards such as FSSAI, HACCP and ISO 22000; continuously improve through training, internal audits and customer feedback; and uphold integrity, impartiality and confidentiality in every engagement.

Our Story

A Young Company, Built on Decades of Expertise

AYINI India was founded in 2025 as a home-grown food safety consulting, compliance and research & development enterprise, based in Chennai. While the company itself is young, it was built by pooling 30+ years of cumulative, hands-on experience in food safety, auditing and regulatory practice — a young mind crafting services on deep-rooted expertise.

Today, that combination lets us operate across three integrated arms — Research & Development, Training, and Integrated Food Compliance — serving manufacturers, hospitality groups, retailers and export businesses across India.
